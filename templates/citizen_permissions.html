Permissions & Privacy

This page describes what data the portal uses and how it is shared with departments.

  • Your name, contact number and location are shared with the selected department only for resolving your complaint.
  • Aadhar number is stored securely and not shown to departments.
  • Complaint text is used by the ML model to route to the correct department.
  • In production, this page can offer more granular controls (notifications, SMS/Email opt-out, etc.).
